Help Centre

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common banking questions. Can't find what you need? Contact our support team.

Accounts

Account Queries

How do I open a Stackly savings account?

▼

Opening is 100% digital. Visit our signup page, provide your full name, email, mobile and PAN/Aadhaar details. Complete Video KYC (takes 5 minutes) and your account is activated instantly. No branch visit, no physical documents needed.

What is the minimum balance requirement?

▼

Digital Savings Account: Zero minimum balance, completely free. Classic Savings: ₹500 (semi-urban) or ₹1,000 (metro/urban). Premium Savings: ₹5,000. Non-maintenance charge is ₹100–200/quarter.

How do I update my KYC documents?

▼

Login to internet banking or the mobile app → My Profile → Update KYC. Upload new Aadhaar, PAN or address proof. Video verification may be required. Processing takes 24–48 business hours.

Can I have multiple accounts at Stackly?

▼

Yes. You can have up to 3 savings accounts (one Digital, one Classic, one Premium), a current account, and multiple FDs. All accounts are accessible from a single login on the app or internet banking.

How do I close my Stackly account?

▼

Submit a written request at any branch or email closure@Stackly.bank from your registered email. Ensure all standing instructions are cancelled and balance is withdrawn before submitting the closure request.

Transfers

Transfer Questions

What are the transfer limits for IMPS/NEFT/RTGS?

▼

IMPS: ₹5 Lakhs per transaction, ₹25 Lakhs daily. NEFT: ₹10 Lakhs per transaction. RTGS: ₹2 Lakhs minimum, no upper limit. UPI: ₹1 Lakh per transaction. These limits can be enhanced by visiting a branch.

How long does a transfer take?

▼

IMPS: Instant 24/7 including holidays. NEFT: Within 30 minutes during banking hours. RTGS: Real-time, same working day. Stackly-to-Stackly: Instant always. International wire: 2–5 business days.

What do I do if a transaction is debited but not credited?

▼

Failed transactions are automatically reversed within 48–72 hours. If not received after 72 hours, call 1800-123-4567 with the transaction reference number. We will initiate a chargeback and resolve within 7 working days.

How do I add a new beneficiary?

▼

Login → Transfer → Add Beneficiary → Enter bank account and IFSC details → Confirm with OTP. New beneficiaries have a 4-hour cooling period before large transfers are allowed for security.

Loans

Loan Questions

What documents are needed for a personal loan?

▼

Salaried: PAN, Aadhaar, last 3 months salary slips, 6 months bank statement, Form 16. Self-employed: PAN, Aadhaar, ITR last 2 years, business proof, 12 months bank statement. Pre-approved loans for existing customers require minimal documentation.

How do I check my loan eligibility?

▼

Use our online EMI Calculator on the Loans page, or call 1800-123-4567. Eligibility depends on income, credit score (CIBIL ≥ 700 recommended), employment stability and existing debt obligations.

Can I prepay my loan early?

▼

Yes. For floating-rate loans, there is zero prepayment charge. For fixed-rate loans, a 2% prepayment charge applies after 6 months. Part-prepayment (minimum 3 EMIs) is also allowed. Contact your relationship manager to initiate.

How do I get a loan repayment certificate or NOC?

▼

After full repayment, NOC is emailed within 7 working days automatically. To request earlier or get a copy, login → My Loans → Request NOC, or visit any branch with your loan account number and identity proof.

Security

Security Questions

Is my money safe with Stackly?

▼

Absolutely. Stackly is regulated by the Reserve Bank of India. Deposits are insured up to ₹5 Lakhs per depositor under DICGC. We use 256-bit SSL encryption, multi-factor authentication and AI-powered fraud detection.

How do I reset my internet banking password?

▼

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered mobile or email and verify via OTP. Set a new strong password. The process takes under 2 minutes and is available 24/7 without contacting support.

I received a suspicious SMS/call claiming to be Stackly. What should I do?

▼

Stackly will NEVER ask for your password, OTP, CVV or full card number via phone or SMS. Hang up immediately and report to our fraud hotline: 1800-123-4999. Do not share any sensitive information with unverified callers.

How do I report an unauthorized transaction?

▼

Immediately call 1800-123-4999 (24/7 fraud hotline) or use the "Emergency Block" in the app. Report within 3 days for maximum protection. We have zero-liability policy for genuine fraud reported promptly.
