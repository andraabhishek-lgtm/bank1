Our Story

About Stackly Bank

20 years of banking excellence, innovation and unwavering commitment to our customers.

Our Foundation

Built on Trust & Technology

Founded in 2005 by visionary banker Rajendra Stackly, Stackly Bank began with a simple mission: to democratize banking in India. From a single branch in Mumbai, we have grown into one of India's leading digital banking platforms serving over 2 million customers across 250+ branches nationwide.

Today, Stackly combines the stability of a traditional bank with the agility of a fintech company — delivering cutting-edge digital products while maintaining the personal relationships that define great banking.

Our Values

What Drives Us

Our core values guide every decision, product and customer interaction at Stackly Bank.

🤝

Trust First

Every product, service and decision starts with the question: "Does this build or preserve customer trust?" Trust is non-negotiable.

🚀

Innovation Always

We invest heavily in R&D to bring banking technology that makes your life simpler, faster and smarter. Never settling for "good enough."

🌍

Inclusive Banking

Banking should be for everyone. We design products that serve rural India as powerfully as urban metros — no one left behind.

🌿

Sustainability

Committed to paperless banking, green offices and financing ESG projects. Banking responsibly for the planet's future.

⚖️

Transparency

No hidden fees, no fine print surprises. We communicate clearly, fairly and completely with every customer.

💡

Customer First

Every feature we build, every policy we create — it all starts and ends with the customer experience in mind.
