Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common banking queries.

How do I open a Stackly savings account?

▼

Opening a savings account is completely digital. Click "Open Account", fill your details, complete KYC with Aadhaar/PAN, and your account is activated within 10 minutes. No branch visit needed.

What documents are needed for a personal loan?

▼

You need PAN card, Aadhaar card, last 3 months salary slips and 6 months bank statement. For self-employed individuals, ITR of last 2 years is required instead of salary slips.

Is my money safe with Stackly?

▼

Absolutely. Stackly is RBI-regulated with deposits insured up to ₹5 Lakhs under DICGC. We use 256-bit encryption, multi-factor authentication and AI fraud detection to keep your funds secure.

How long does a fund transfer take?

▼

IMPS transfers are instant 24/7. NEFT processes in under 30 minutes during banking hours. RTGS is real-time for amounts above ₹2 Lakhs. Inter-bank Stackly transfers are immediate.

How do I reset my internet banking password?

▼

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered mobile/email, verify via OTP, and set a new password. The process takes less than 2 minutes and is available 24/7.

What is the minimum balance for a savings account?

▼

Stackly offers zero-balance Digital Savings Accounts. For Classic Savings, the minimum balance is ₹500 in semi-urban areas and ₹1,000 in metro/urban areas.
